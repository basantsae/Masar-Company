Al Reem Island

Al Reem Island is one of the fastest-developing neighbourhoods in Abu Dhabi. Dotted with several residential and retail options, the island has become one of the most in-demand locations in Abu Dhabi in a short span of time. Potential residents have an array of property types on Al Reem Island Abu Dhabi to choose from – luxury penthouses, apartments and stunning waterfront villas.

Al Saadiyat Island

Al Saadiyat Island is a tourism-cultural project for nature and Emirati heritage and culture. The project, under development, consists of a large, low-lying island. A mixed commercial, residential, and leisure project is currently under construction on the island. Saadiyat Island is expected to become Abu Dhabi's cultural centre, mostly for the Island’s Cultural District that is expected to include eight museums.

Rahman Island

Ramhan Island Beautiful, raw and untouched, Ramhan is a naturally shaped heavenly island, located in Abu Dhabi. It carries all the charm of nature from bays and mangrove trees. The incredibly designed masterplan offers waterfront luxury villas with high-end amenities, a world-class Marina with state-of-the-art shopping and fine dining experiences, unrivaled hospitality with a luxurious hotel and serviced residence, besides a tranquil wellness center amidst serene surroundings and open bay views.

Bloom Gardens

Bloom Gardens located on Al Salam Street is a master project by Bloom Holding. It is one of the most popular residential destinations in the capital city, mainly due to a variety of detached villas. More importantly, it lies on Sheikh Zayed Bin Sultan Street which is a major highway in Abu Dhabi running across the residential and commercial districts. With Spanish and Italian-style villas and townhouses, the community offers a range of affordable accommodation plans for couples and families alike. This self-sustained district is planned to provide all the facilities within the boundaries. It already has a community clubhouse and playgrounds.

Yas Island

Yas Island is an island in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. The island’s development project was initiated in 2006 by Abu Dhabi-based Aldar Properties, with the aim of turning the island into a multi-purpose leisure, shopping and entertainment center at an estimated total cost of over billion.

Al Jubail Island

Al Jubail Island RESIDENTIAL COMMUNITY Spread across 5,000 hectares of coastal estuaries and mangrove forests, Al Jubail Island by LEAD Developments is one of the newest waterfront developments in the capital city. Visitors are more familiar with the area as it features the famous Jubail Mangrove Park and Mangrove Walk. Other than the beautiful mangrove reserves, the area has more to offer. Nestled between Saadiyat Island and Yas Island, Jubail Island is a stunning natural retreat located minutes away from the bustling city centre.

Ghantoot

Ghantoot is a large coastal area located between Abu Dhabi and Dubai. The area is known for housing Ghantoot Racing & Polo Club and the Ghantoot Marina. It will be home to luxurious villas built in contemporary design along the coastline of Sahel Al Emarat. Masder

Masdar City is an urban community in Abu Dhabi, the capital of the United Arab Emirates. It was built by Masdar, a subsidiary of the state-owned Mubadala Investment Company, with the majority of seed capital provided by the Government of Abu Dhabi

Al Ghadeer

Al Ghadeer is a rapidly growing community by ALDAR, a leading real estate developer in Abu Dhabi. Nestling on the Dubai-Abu Dhabi border, it comprises low-rise residential buildings and villas. The community is a perfect match for those wanting to work in Dubai while living in Abu Dhabi. However, the location might not be the best fit for people who like short commutes since Al Ghadeer is slightly away from key districts of both emirates. AL Ghadeer is one of the popular areas for buying affordable apartments in Abu Dhabi.

Al Gurm

Al Gurm is a luxurious residential and tourist project in Abu Dhabi. Master developer Aldar has put a lot of consideration into building this community and making sure that the properties are unique from other waterfront communities of UAE. The developers have worked closely with the Urban Planning Council to ensure the community strictly adheres to the 2030 development plan for Abu Dhabi. Al Gurm focuses on preserving the natural resources that make Abu Dhabi unique as it is nestled among mangrove forests. The main appeal of the area is the waterfront lifestyle, luxurious amenities and the Al Gurm Resort.

Al Raha Beach

Al Raha Beach, a vibrant community, offers luxurious waterfront living at its best, boasting an impressive 5.2 million sq. m. of natural beach shoreline. Al Raha Beach is one of the popular areas to rent high-end apartments in Abu Dhabi. Boasting a premium mix of residential and commercial properties, the development is also home to the top-favorite's tourist attraction, Al Raha Beach Hotel. This area guide explores how Raha Beach offers a balanced lifestyle to those seeking a deluxe community that’s complete with entertainment, culture and world-class amenities.
