              Explore The Most Desired Communties in Abu Dhabi

                Explore Abu Dhabi Regions

                • Al Mushrif

                  Al Mushrif is a central neighborhood in the city of Abu Dhabi zone one. Al-Mushrif is located in an area between Sheikh Rashid Bin Saeed Street "Airport Road" and Arabian gulf Street, which extends to Mussafah Bridge. It contains the Al Mushrif Palace and Al Mushrif Park.

                • Al Murror

                  Al Muroor gets its name from the famous, Sultan Bin Zayed, The First Street, it was formerly known as Muroor Road or 4th Street. The neighborhood houses a mix of commercial and residential properties. Presenting a quiet yet spirited environment for the residents,Al Muroor is one of the best areas for singles to rent property in Abu Dhabi.

                • Al Bateen

                  Al Bateen Upscale Al Bateen is the home of high-rise hotels overlooking the Persian Gulf, with family beaches lining the elegant Corniche promenade. Photographs and documents displayed in Zayed Heritage Center celebrate the life of Sheikh Zayed, the former ruler of Abu Dhabi. Superyachts moor in Marsa Al Bateen Marina, which is surrounded by stylish global restaurants.

                • Al Karama

                  Al Karama is located in Abu Dhabi City, almost in the centre of the island. The area lies between two blocks called Al Karamah Street and Sheikh Rashid Bin Saeed Street, and is closed off at either end by Mohamed Bin Khalifa Street and Hazza Bin Zayed Street

                • Al Zaab

                  Al Zaab Right in the center of the capital city, Al Zaab is a residential community in Abu Dhabi preferred by families and individuals. It is located between Al Karamah and Mubarak Bin Mohammed Street. The area offers many apartments and a variety of villas as well. The residents of the area benefit from the facilities available in Al Zaab.

                • Air port Road

                  Air port Road Airport Street has many names in the emirate such as Airport Road, 2nd Street or its formal name Sheikh Rashid Bin Saeed Al Maktoum Street. It is a key point for the emirate as Abu Dhabi is split down the middle by Airport Road, connecting the entire capital together.

                • Al Danah

                  Al Danah is located in the heart of Abu Dhabi one can spot it near the Abu Dhabi Mall and Al Manhal Palace. It is rather a bustling suburb with streets loaded with shops, restaurants and other amenities. One of the oldest areas of Abu Dhabi, Al Danah is home to Madinat Zayed Shopping Centre

                • Al Hosn

                  Al Hosn, is a popular area in Abu Dhabi that lies on the northern side of the capital city. The district is located towards the coastal shore, western side of Al Danah and on the east of Khalidiya Village. It is home to Qasr Al Hosn, a historical landmark of the emirate.

                • Al Khaleej Al Arabi Street

                  Al Khaleej Al Arabi Street is part of the large mixed-use locality in Al Bateen, a key residential community in Abu Dhabi. It is filled with landscaped gardens and master developments. Most restaurants, hotels and bars are just a few minutes away.

                • Al Khaldiya

                  Khalidiya or its official name Al Khalidiyah is a cultural hub of Abu Dhabi. Khalidiyah is where old meets new while also showcasing the capitals heritage. Its central location makes this vibrant suburb one of the most popular areas for expats and local families looking to live right in the heart of Abu Dhabi.

                • Al Maharba

                  Al Maharba Abu Dhabi, the capital of the United Arab Emirates, sits off the mainland on an island in the Persian (Arabian) Gulf. Its focus on oil exports and commerce is reflected by the skylines modern towers and shopping megacenters such as Abu Dhabi and Marina malls.

                • Al Manaseer

                  Al Manaseer is part of the Al Rowdah district and lies between the popular residential areas of Al Karamah and Al Bateen. It is a peaceful, family-friendly area. There are many apartments and a few villas in the small and cosy locality. The area is equally popular among families and individuals because of its location near the well-connected districts of the capital city.

                • Al Manhal

                  Al Manhal is a popular residential community located in the heart of the UAE capital. It is home to historical landmarks including Al Manhal Palace and Al Bateen Palace. Al Manhal has apartment buildings as well as villas. Amenities such as schools, parks, hospitals, supermarkets, shopping malls, hotels and leisure places are in proximity.

                • Al Marina

                  Fairmont Marina Residences conveys elegance and luxury. A wonderful opportunity to acquire, imagine your residences terrace opening to the breathtaking, panoramic view of Abu Dhabi. Located next to Marina Sunset Bay Villas and the upcoming Fairmont Hotel and within walking distance to Marina Mall.

                • Al Nahyan

                  Al Nahyan It is has become a central part of Abu Dhabi's rental real estate landscape. Located in the heart of the city, the area is home to the Al Nahyan Stadium and neighbours the Al Karamah and Al Mushrif districts. The area is still close enough for those who commute to the city center.

                • Al Wahdah

                  Al Wahdah is best known for Al Wahda Sports Club, Al Nahyan Stadium and Al Wahda Mall. The iconic community of Al Wahdah in Abu Dhabi is home to a number of mid and high-rise buildings along with a few residential villas. It offers a buzzing lifestyle to its residents because of its central location in the capital city.

                • Al Zahyia

                  Al Zahiyah, formerly known as the Tourist Club Area, is an area in central Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. Al Zahiyah is an entertainment location, with restaurants, hotels (including the Beach Rotana), bars, and nightlife. The Abu Dhabi Mall is also located here.

                • Corniche

                  Corniche Area is one of the most sought after places to live in Abu Dhabi, offering luxury apartments close to the beach. Corniche Area is known to be one of the few areas that offers waterfront living in residential towers. The area is close to the popular Corniche Road, which is considered one of the most visited tourist areas in Abu Dhabi.

                • Khalifa Park

                  Khalifa Park Where theres a lot to do in Khalifa Park with kids and friends, the place is ideal for renting properties in Abu Dhabi. Also, tenants lie close to basic living amenities including schools, pharmacies, medical centers and eateries. Khalifa Park is somewhere between Al Bateen Executive Airport and Eastern Mangrove.

                • Tourist Club Area

                  Tourist Club Area is now known by its new name Al Zahiyah. The area is located on the eastern side of Abu Dhabi. It is one of the downtown areas of the capital city and enjoys a prime location by being in the centre of Corniche Road East and Al Falah Street. The community has high-rise buildings and towers and there are no villas in the area.

                • Zayed Sports City

                  Zayed Sports City is the premier sport and entertainment venue in the region. Built to fulfil the vision of the late Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan, the UAEs founding father and first president, ZSC continues to build, learn and grow, ensuring it delivers the best possible experience to every guest.
